The Core Architecture

The Decision Loop

Every decision in Delphi travels through seven stages — not as a linear checklist, but as a continuous, adaptive cycle. Each pass tightens understanding, reduces uncertainty, and improves the next round of judgment.

Cognitive Layer

Specialized Reasoning Agents

Delphi doesn't use a single general-purpose AI. Instead, each cognitive function is handled by a dedicated agent — trained for a specific type of reasoning. They work in parallel, cross-verify each other, and hand off to humans at the right moments.

Authority Architecture

Mission Control Layers

Delphi separates execution from strategy from governance. Each layer has distinct authority, tools, and responsibilities — eliminating the confusion of who should decide what.

EMC GOVERNS AUTHORITY

GK LEARNS & ADJUSTS
The Governance Kernel tracks calibration, debt, and drift — continuously improving the system's judgment quality.
